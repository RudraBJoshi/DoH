## UESL Game Maker

Design your own game, then play it! This tool was created for UESL students to express creativity while learning game design concepts in a fully accessible environment.

---

### How to Use the Game Maker

#### 🎨 Design Tab — Build Your Level

Use the **tool palette** on the left to place objects onto the canvas:

| Tool | What it does |
|------|-------------|
| 🖱️ **Select** | Click any object to select it; drag to move it |
| 🎮 **Player Start** | Click to set where the player begins |
| 🧱 **Draw Wall** | Click + drag to draw a wall or barrier |
| ⭐ **Place Star** | Click to place a collectible star |
| 👾 **Place NPC** | Click once for the NPC's position, click again for its patrol endpoint |
| 🗑️ **Erase** | Click any object to delete it |

The **Properties Panel** on the right updates based on what you have selected or what tool is active — use it to change wall colors, NPC speed, NPC emoji faces, and barrier labels.

**Actions:**
- **↩ Undo / ↪ Redo** — step back and forward through your changes (`Ctrl+Z` / `Ctrl+Y`)
- **🗑 Clear All** — wipe the level and start fresh
- **💾 Save** — saves your level to the browser (survives page refreshes)
- **📂 Load** — loads your previously saved level
- **▶ Play!** — jump straight into your level

---

#### ▶ Play Tab — Play Your Game

Collect **all the stars** to win! Controls:

- **Arrow Keys / WASD** — move the player
- **R** — restart the level
- In **Single-Button Mode**: `SPACE` cycles direction, `ENTER` moves

---

#### ⚙️ Settings Tab — Accessibility Options

All settings can be toggled at any time and take effect immediately in Play mode:

| Setting | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| 🐌 Slow Mode | Halves game speed — gives more reaction time |
| 🕹️ Single-Button Mode | Two-key play: SPACE cycles direction, ENTER moves |
| ⚡ High-Contrast | Black background, bright yellow & white — visual processing support |
| 🔍 Large Sprites | Enlarges player, NPCs, and stars |
| 🛑 Reduce Motion | Removes particle effects and screen flash |
| 🧭 Guided Mode | Dashed arrow guides to nearest star; NPCs become friendly |
| 🔊 Sound Effects | Collect tones, hit sound, win jingle |
| 🎉 Celebrations | Particle bursts on star collection |
