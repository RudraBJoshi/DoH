The Unified Esports League
Esports + Technology Program

The Foundation for Unified Esports supports the function of the Unified Esports League in its many efforts to host and coordinate esports experiences for individuals with intellectual and developmental disabilities.

UESL's events and programs are built for more than just competition. These events increase opportunity for individuals to create a career and college pathway in the gaming + STEM industries.

Inclusive Design

Built for Every Student

🕹️

Simplified Controls

Single-button or minimal-input configurations. Slow mode reduces game speed giving every student more time to react.

👁️

Visual Adjustments

High-contrast color schemes, reduced animations, and larger sprites support students with visual processing differences.

🗺️

Guided Mode

NPCs assist rather than challenge. Colors, arrows, and markers clearly guide students through each level.

🔊

Audio Feedback

Configurable audio cues and celebration animations. Instructors can toggle audio on or off per student's needs.

⚙️

Adjustable Difficulty

Easy, medium, and hard tiers. NPC speed, barrier density, and movement sensitivity are all independently adjustable.

🎉

Reward Animations

Flashing scores and celebration effects give real-time positive reinforcement as students progress through levels.
